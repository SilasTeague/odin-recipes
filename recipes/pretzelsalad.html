Pretzel Salad

Description

mmm mm MMMMM I just wanna take a bite of that scrumptious strawberry goodness.

Ingredients

  • Crushed Pretzels (2 Cups)
  • Margarine (1 stick)
  • Sugar (1 cup, 2 tablespoons)
  • Strawberry Jell-O (6 oz)
  • Frozen Strawberries (20 oz)
  • Water (2 cups)
  • Cream Cheese (8oz)
  • Cool Whip (8oz)

Steps

  1. Bake crushed pretzels, melted margarine, and 2 tablespoons of sugar at 200 degrees for 10 minutes.
  2. Chill hot water, strawberry jello, and hot water in a bowl for 2 hours.
  3. Thoroughly blend cream cheese, 1 cup sugar, and cool whip.
  4. Spread this mixture over the pretzels being careful to seal the edges.
  5. Pour thickened strawberries over this and let sit until firm.
  6. Enjoy!
